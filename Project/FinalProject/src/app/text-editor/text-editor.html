What's This About?

This text editor lets you manipulate text in various ways. Because sometimes you need your text to SHOUT, whisper, or do that irritating alternating case thing that makes you look like you're stuck in 2005.

Built using two-way data binding with ngModel, string manipulation methods, and a healthy dose of "why not?" It keeps a history of your last 10 operations, so you can track your descent into text transformation madness.
